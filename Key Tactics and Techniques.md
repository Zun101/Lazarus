# 🔍 Key Tactics and Techniques

- 🎯 **Spear Phishing**: Targeted attacks via realistic, deceptive emails to infiltrate networks.
- 🔗 **Supply Chain Attacks**: Compromising trusted software updates or third-party providers.
- 🛠️ **Zero-Day Exploits**: Using unknown vulnerabilities to bypass secure systems.
- 💸 **Cryptojacking & Ransomware**: Secretly mining cryptocurrency and extorting funds.

Lazarus's sophistication in these areas makes them highly dangerous, especially given their relentless attacks and adaptability in response to new cybersecurity measures.
