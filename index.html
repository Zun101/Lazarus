💥 Lazarus Group: A Persistent and Sophisticated Cyber Threat

The Lazarus Group is a highly organized hacking collective, widely believed to be state-sponsored with links to North Korea.

🌐 Targets

  • 🏛️ Governments
  • 💰 Financial Institutions
  • 🏥 Healthcare
  • 💻 Cryptocurrency Platforms

Their advanced tactics, persistence, and adaptability make Lazarus one of the most enduring and dangerous cyber threats.

Understanding the methods and motivations of the Lazarus Group is crucial for developing effective cybersecurity measures and strategies.
